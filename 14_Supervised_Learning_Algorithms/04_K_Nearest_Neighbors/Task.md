# Task: Fruit Classification

## Objective
Classify fruit based on mass and width.

## Data
*   **Mass**: [150, 170, 80, 75, 200]
*   **Width**: [7.5, 8.0, 6.0, 5.8, 8.5]
*   **Label**: ['Apple', 'Apple', 'Lemon', 'Lemon', 'Apple']

## Tasks
1.  **Train**: Fit a KNN classifier with **k=1**.
2.  **Predict**: A fruit with mass **160g** and width **7.0cm**. Is it an Apple or Lemon?
